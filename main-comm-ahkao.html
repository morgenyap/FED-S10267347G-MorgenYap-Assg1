Ah Kao

Role: Student Advisor

Year: Y3

Course: Diploma in Aerospace Engineering (DipAEG)

School: School of Engineering (SoE)

Main Responsibilities

As Student Advisor, Ah Kao's main responsibilities include providing support to the Main Committee along their journey and giving advice.

Fun Facts

  • Ah Kao dislikes strawberries 🍓 and strawberry-flavored food, as he always says there’s a “grass taste” in strawberries!
  • He enjoys cooking during his free time 👨‍🍳 and loves photographing memories 📸. (The airport is his comfort place!! ✈️ - true Aerospace student indeed!)
  • Ah Kao's favorite food is Hotplate Tofu! He loves anything tofu-like—"Mostly the egg tofu kind," as he calls it.
  • He also loves Katsu Don (because of the half-soggy and fried chicken drenched in sauce), Mala, and Chili Oil Dumplings! 🥟

Why He Joined FoodAID

Ah Kao joined FoodAID because he always enjoyed giving back to the community. Besides, he loves food and believes in "Living to Eat"! 🍴 He wanted to share the joy of food with everyone and met very welcoming and friendly people in the CCA, which motivated him to contribute more. 😄

Favorite Principle

While he doesn't have a specific quote 🎤 he resonates with, Ah Kao always observes this principle: "Do your best in everything you do." 😎
