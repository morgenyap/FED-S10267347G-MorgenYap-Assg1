Main Responsibilities

As the Vice-President, Shabera's responsibilities include:

  • Vetting and submitting proposals together with Ryan and Hema 😆.
  • Ensuring the welfare of committee members by organising bonding events, such as committee dinners and outings 🍽️💜.

Why She Joined FoodAID

Shabera joined FoodAID because she wanted a platform to volunteer and give back to society 🫶🏾. Before joining FoodAID, she didn't have many opportunities to volunteer regularly. She also found FoodAID to be a place where she could connect with people who shared her passion for food 🍜🍔.

Favorite Principle

A quote Shabera firmly believes in is: "She believed she could, so she did!" According to her, when you wholeheartedly invest your best effort into something, regardless of the outcome, you will find contentment and satisfaction 💪🏾😎.
